Bernese Mountain Dog

Country: Switzerland

Behavior: Gentle, affectionate, good-natured

Nutrition: High-quality dog food with a focus on joint health.

Overview: Bernese Mountain Dogs are known for their gentle and affectionate nature. They are great with families and enjoy being part of household activities. Regular exercise and a balanced diet are important for their well-being.
